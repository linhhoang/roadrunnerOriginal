IT Staff - Nhân Viên Công Nghệ Thông Tin

Vietnam Float Glass Company Ltd. - Công Ty Liên Doanh Kính Nổi Việt Nam Head Office: Phuong Lieu commune, Que Vo district, Bac Ninh province Website: http://www.vfg.vn

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

Job Description: 1. Install and configure computer hardware, software, systems, networks, photocopiers, printers, fax machines, scanners 2. Plan and undertake scheduled maintenance upgrades 3. Respond to breakdowns 4. Investigate, diagnose and solve computer software and hardware faults 5. Repair equipment and replace parts 6. Implement procurement of hardware, software and other IT equipment as required; Manage stocks of IT equipment, consumables and other supplies 7. Maintain records of software licences 8. Arrange control system of electronic documents/records in the Company’s servers and ensure their security and secret 9. Performs other duties as assigned or as the situation dictates.

Job Requirement

Requirements: - Male; University graduated in IT field - At least 3 years working experiences in IT field - Knowledge of IT systems and the relevant laws governing them - Skill to set up, operate, maintain and repair IT systems - Having MCSA or MCSE Certificate is an advantage - Ability to communicate and present in English - On-call work may be required. Successful candidates will be worked in ideal working environment together with: - Attractive salary & allowances - 13th month salary on Tet holiday - 1st half and 2nd half bonuses - Celebration allowance on Public Holidays - Full statutory rights. Interested candidates should submit their Application Letter and CV (in English), copies of diplomas, documents proving working experience, medical certificate and latest photos to the Company Head Office or through the email address of the Company’s Human Resources Division (which can be seen at the Company's website: http://www.vfg.vn) no later than 10th August 2014.
