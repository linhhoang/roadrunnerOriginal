Job Description

This position reports to the Country Manager of ARINC Vietnam and duties include but are not limited to the following: • Successful candidate is responsible for overseeing and the management of the airport systems solutions that may be deployed at the site • He/She has to administer these systems, ensuring system availability to airport customers on a 24/7 basis while meeting strict service level agreements in accordance with the contract with the customer • Candidate will supervise a team of site administrators and technical staff (sub-contractors) to ensure efficient operation of the site and serve as the main point of contact between ARINC and our customers, both internal and external • He/She will interact with clients, vendors, purchasing, engineering and ARINC internal parties • He/She will coordinate and liaise with airport and airline staff in the planning and execution phases of project and support proposal development efforts • This position will also act as a liaison between client and engineering staff, develop budgets, requirements statements, and schedules for Airports projects based on customer needs • The ideal candidate will involve travel to the site (when required) during planning and execution phase, provide overall direction and on site guidance as well as fulfill ISO procedural and documentation requirements • He/She will act as the technical interface to ARINC and provide 3rd Level (on-call engineering) support for existing operational airport systems in the Noi Bai Airport, Ha Noi (HAN) • Reports to the airport authority and the airline station manager/representative in terms of operational matters • Is responsible for the site team of technicians and for necessary escalation of service issues • Ensures that all site technicians are trained to the required levels and maintaining staffing levels and for hiring technicians • Works closely with the HAN project team to ensure that support processes reflect the needs of the airport authority • Duties include, without limitation, planning, arrangement and supervision of the production support maintenance, Planned Maintenance, Corrective Maintenance, other services and Improvement Works, fault analyses and overall management of the provisions in this Maintenance Plan

Job Requirement

• Ten (10) or more years technical experience preferably in an airport environment • Five (5) years or more progressive experience in project lifecycle, including at least one large scale airport project • Experience with airport operations and relevant IT systems including Common Use systems and Network • Ability to write technical reports, develop schedules, and analyze financial reports is required • Previous experience preparing and delivering executive level briefings • Demonstrated written and oral communication skills (Both Vietnamese and English) • Strong client relationship skills and strong organizational skills to direct technical team • Independent and has proven track records in people management / leadership functions
