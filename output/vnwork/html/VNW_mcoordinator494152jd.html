Continuous Improvement Program Coordinator

Detmold Packaging VIETNAM Số 9, đường 4,KCN Việt Nam Singapore 1, Thuận An, Bình Dương.

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

POSITION OBJECTIVE Deliver the continuous improvement program for Detmold Packaging Vietnam (DPV) Operations. Responsible for driving the required CIP results through analysis and action as well as teaching, facilitating and mentoring manufacturing facilities in Vietnam to implement the tools and concept of continuous improvement. Lead all matters relating to technical issues in collaboration with all other stakeholders for DPV. POSITION DUTIES Assist with the development and expansion of the continuous improvement process across the Vietnam Manufacturing facilities, particularly supporting improvement teams. Drive the continuous improvement process and cost reduction plans across the manufacturing facilities in Vietnam. Evaluate processes systematically to identify opportunities for through put increases and quality improvements. Analyze manufacturing waste and efficiency and develop improvement strategies for implementation Determine Process capabilities and develop improvement plans as needed. Improve procedures to make the most efficient use of resources in the manufacture of products. Assist in the development and deployment of operator training. Take an active role in improving plant safety, food safety, and food security with an emphasis on eliminating hazards. Cascade CIP target to individual plant CIP and assist Maintenance and Manufacturing managers achieve their yearly target. Responsible for defining, implementing, adapting and optimizing the manufacturing process. Develop training materials for CIP toolbox tools and drive the use of these across operations. Facilitate problem solving methodologies Responsible for CIP verification and reporting. Coordinates and conducts trials to assess process or material capability and writes and distributes trial reports. Effectively communicate with all departments with regards to Processes, Materials and Machinery improvements. Takes a lead role in developing, documenting and implementing fast changeover techniques for all equipment. Takes a lead role in improving Detmold Packaging Vietnam’s print capability though training delivery and liaison with local and group personnel.

Job Requirement

Degree qualified with over 5 years of experience working in a manufacturing environment, involved in process improvements or engineering, preferably from packaging industry. Good knowledge of printing (preferably flexographic) ,TQC, lean manufacturing and quality management would be advantageous.
