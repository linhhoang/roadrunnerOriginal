IT Engineer

Taisei - Vinaconex JV Noi Bai International Airport Terminal 2 Construction Site- Noi Bai

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

• Working for our Noibai International Aiport - Terminal 2 Construction Project located in Socson district, Hanoi (daily means of transport from Hanoi to the Project site will be provided by the company). • Job performance shall be related to the Airport Information System, Flight Information System, etc… - To participate in negotiations with IT Vendors. - To review material submittal and its compliance statement before forwarding to the Consultant for approval - To review shop drawings before submission to the Consultant - To prepare PQP/ITP in coordination with the Project requirements. - To prepare Method Statement in coordination with the Project requirements with the help of specifications, drawings and contract documents. - Checklist preparation. - Daily Site inspection with the Consultants. - MIR Site rising, maintaining logs and visiting store with the Consultant for permanent material inspection. - To perform coordination for internal quality audit and closing the NCR's in coordination with the Management Representative/Quality Manager. - To coordinate with Manager in Quality related matters. - To monitor subcontractor's activity and to issue NCR if required. - To ensure all testing instruments are standardized, calibrated and maintain the log. - To follow up with the Consultant’s NCR, Deficiency Notices and to close it as soon as possible. - To follow up the Consultant snags/client snags/internal snags with the site engineers and maintain the log. - To prepare and follow up for the close out documents. - More details will be discussed in the interview.

Job Requirement

• Vietnamese citizen • Bachelor/ Master Degree in IT field. • Having at least 05 years of experience in IT field (candidates with experience related to Airport Information System, Flight Information System will be preferable). • Good command of English (communication and technical document reading) • Having working experience for Japanese company and international company is an advantage. • Positive for new job field. Further Information: • All application documents including C.V must be in English. • Salary by negotiation. • Full-time employment. • Telephone contact is not permitted.
