An Architect To Work As Project Coordinator

Nikken Sekkei Ltd Suite 505, Block B, Indochina Park Tower, 04 Nguyen Dinh Chieu St., Dist. 1, HCMC

Apply Now

Job Description

Nikken Sekkei Ltd is the lead architect providing a full design services package to a Vietnam’s leading commercial bank’s Headquarters in the heart of Hochiminh City. The project will soon be started in August 2014 and is anticipated to take several years. Nikken Sekkei Ltd shall work with the Client, the Client’s Project Manager (an American PM), and Nikken’s key local consultants including ARUP, PTW, etc. As the only Project Coordinator for this prominent project, you are responsible to: 1. Coordinate with the Client / Project Manager / Sub-consultants / Local authorities; 2. Attend periodical meetings with Client / Project Manager / Sub-consultants / Local authorities and prepare minutes of meeting; 3. Interpret in meetings with Client when and as required; 4. Translate documents (Vietnamese-English-Vietnamese) including official letters, referential documents, emails, etc. 5. Prepare official correspondences to related parties (usually to Client and authorities); 6. Handle submission documents to Client / Project Manager / Local authorities; 7. Handle applications to authorities for administrative permits (work permit, etc.) – with assistance of Nikken Sekkei’s Representative Office; 8. Conduct simple book-keeping of monthly expenses of the Project Office; 9. Coordinate with Nikken Sekkei’s Osaka and Tokyo offices in hotel and ticket reservation, and any other administration task required; 10. Assist the Project Manager and other Design Team Members in other tasks required for the project.

Job Requirement

1. Job requirements: • A competent architect • At least 03 years experience in large-scale international projects. • Able to work under high pressure independently and also as part of a team. • Having a good overview of project, being able to manage tight deadlines. • Good communication skills, highly self-motivated, independent. * Only qualified candidates should apply. * CV must be in English. 2. Job offers: • Monthly net salary: USD 900 – USD 1,000 •Transportation provided for business purposes, • Phone allowance provided, • 13th month salary, • Yearly salary review, • Annual leaves, • Work at: HCMC Office • Basic working time: Mon - Fri, 08:00am - 12:00am, 01:00pm - 05:00pm • Starting time: 4th week of August, 2014. Application deadline: 22 August 2014 (but can be closed earlier as soon as the hiring process is successful)
