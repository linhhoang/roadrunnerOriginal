Job Description

* Develop web applications for the company owned services and Japanese clients. * Type of system can be from B2C online shop to B2B enterprise system. So you will have plenty of experiences with new technologies and methodologies. * Basic technology we use (you will be trained for them) + JavaScript / CSS / HTMLX for client (browser) side. + Ruby on Rails / Java for server side. + MySQL and other appropriate technologies for backend. + Linux as Server OS.

Job Requirement

* Experience in developing web applications. * Fresh graduate with excellent ability and outstanding performance in IT school/training center is also welcome. * Can be work experiences or personal projects but need to understand the general whole process of web applications. * You will be asked for your accomplishment and contributions. * English proficiency. At least basic read/written English. Basic spoken English is preferable. Salary * $300 – $1000 (Negotiable with high skills/experiences) D. BENEFITS * Enjoy insurance and other benefits under the provisions of Vietnam Labor Law. Have annual physical examination, lunch and parking allowances. * Review the salary, based on the working results, and consider the salary adjustmentevery 6 months. Enjoy 13th month salary, bonus from the results of the projects. * Working time: 5 days/week, from 08:00 am to 17:00 pm. Have every-other-week Saturday off (Basically Saturdays will be spent for intense-training, study group and personal projects for skill-up). Sundays and other public holidays are off as prescribed by the State regulations. * Flexible work time, required to adjust the work time according to the progress of tasks to respect the projects deadlines. * Enjoy a creative and high-tech working environment with various tools of communication (ChatWork, Jira, Confluence,Google hangout, Skype,…). * Enjoy high-quality training for future career development. - Intensive training for new employees, equivalent to our training in Japan. + From basic business manner to detailed tech-skills. + You will have full one month of training. (Details you can ask at the interview.) - Business/Communication Skill-up + Professionalism. Sincerity, Punctuality, coping with difficulties, and so on. + How to make full use of your brain. Logical thinking, Analyzing, Hypothesis testing, Problem solving. Presentation with logic. + Project management. Goal sharing, Planning, Tracing the process, Archiving the goal. + Communications. Leadership, Couching. Concern for others and proper self-recognitions. - Technical Skill-up. + Coding Standard with detailed reason why it is needed. + Object Oriented Programming in depth. + Programming Languages and other specifications with very recent techniques Ruby/Rails/Javascript/CSS/XHTML - Language training. + Japanese + English * There are training programs to Japan for the cream of the crop. Enjoy high chance of promotion – high possibility to become the Key person of the company. * Other activities: annual travelling with the company, teambuilding, birthday party,… E. OTHER DETAILS * In CV: Specify the recruitment position: Program Manager (for example). Specify working experiences and educational background especially related to the applying position.
