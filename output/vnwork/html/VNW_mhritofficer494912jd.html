Job Description

Working place: HCMC Department: Organization Development Number of vacancies: 01 Report to: OD Manager -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------Main Duties: • To support BE (HR) in custodian of local Globe-HR and liaise with GC AOA and LGO on Globe-HR issues, including coordination for market role assignment. • To ensure lead time, due date & target achievement, compliance to Nestlé standards & GLOBE requirements, maintain GLOBE BE/SU network and process and support Globe traning • Lead/Handle HR/HRIS related projects such as Kronos, Talent and Performance Management System (TPMS), Learning Management Solution (LMS), Information to insight (i2i) amongst others and working closely with cross-functional teams

Job Requirement

• Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or Information System • 1-2 year experience in Human Resources function, HR Information Systems or Project Implementation • Advanced English Level • Strong knowledge of SAP/HR system • Good communication, interpersonal and presentation skills. • Strong analytical and organization skills, problem solving and decision making skills • Strong service orientation and exceptional action and results-orientation and is a team-player ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------Interested candidates pls apply your CVs to: NESTLÉ VIETNAM Floor 5, Empress Tower, 138-142 Hai Ba Trung,DaKao Ward, District 1, HCMC Attn: Recruitment Team - HR Dept Or click "APPLY" button to submit your applications via email Please include in the email subject the Job title of position you want to apply for.
