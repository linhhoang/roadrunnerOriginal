Job Description

- Design and develop web applications with pixel perfect quality in regards to navigational layouts and appearance - Work directly with the design team and back-end development team to implement mockups using HTML/CSS/JavaScript and integrate with the back-end stack - Debug and troubleshoot layout and scripting issues across a variety of desktop and mobile browsers Opportunities** - Work with a truly global highly experienced engineering team spanning 3 continents across the globe - Excellent salary and employee benefits (high-quality health insurance, bonus programs, employee referral programs, and many more) - Build a solid career path with clear and attractive promotion opportunities - Receive unparalleled coaching and training from industry-leading experts

Job Requirement

- 2+ years experience designing and developing web interfaces - Significant experience in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX, JSON - Familiar with front-end web frameworks such as Bootstrap, AngularJS, Ember.js - Experience with responsive design principles for web sites - Proficiency with Adobe Creative Suite, Photoshop or other image editing software - Experience in agile development disciplines and principles (preferably Scrum) - Working knowledge of Git (or similar version control systems) - Self-motivated, ability to work independently and efficiently with a strong attention to detail and accuracy - Ability to multi-task and work across multiple projects - Excellent written and verbal English skills
