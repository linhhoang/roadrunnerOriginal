Sourcing Manager (garments / Textiles)

VPĐD Gloria Jeans Vietnam Room 16.02, level 16, IPC Tower 1489 Nguyen Van Linh. Tan Phong ward. District 7. HCM city

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

• Experience in outwear, active wear, specialize in winter jacket, woolen clothes,woven,knit. • Responsible for merchandising activities, assisting with purchase order execution. Follow up/manage the orders/fabric specialize in garment • Communicate and handles all style developments for new projects and requirements to factories and all parties concerned • Coordinate daily operational activities in screening new product development samples, track and review pre-production sample, print, embroidery, wash for sampling specs and colour swatches to ensure samples match with required specifications and quality standard. • Negotiate with vendors on CM,CMPT price, discount, shipment and working terms and realize cost saving/quality targets through pro-active vendor management • Monitor production process properly to meet quantity, quality, order specifications and delivery dates. • Diagnose production problems and work with factories to present alternatives to both buyers and management • Assist to implement new purchasing process/organize and maintain all purchasing documentations and documentations and records. • Checking factory’s capacity situation & report to Managers • Check sample consumption, packing and making cost • Ensure to have perfect PP samples for mass production and closely work with production line.

Job Requirement

• Education: Graduated from university • 4-5 years experienced as Merchandiser in the garment industry • Good negotiation skills, able to work under hard pressure • Fluently oral and writing in English. • Good at computer skills (Word, Excel, Power point...) • Hard-working, willing to learn and improve, honest • Healthy to travel to the factory • Documents - Application letter - CV in English - Government ID copy - University degree - Photo 4x6 (1pc).

As of 2012 the company operates over 430 stores in malls and high streets across Russia and the Ukraine. The central office of the Company located in Rostov-on-Don manages seven regional sales offices and 33 factories in Russia and Ukraine that produce over 65,000,000 units of products annually.
